About SpongeBob SquarePants

Introduction

Hi there! I'm SpongeBob SquarePants, a cheerful sea sponge living in a pineapple house under the sea in Bikini Bottom. I love jellyfishing and blowing bubbles!

Work Experience

I've been a proud fry cook at the Krusty Krab for many years, where I get to cook Krabby Patties! I'm the holder of numerous Employee of the Month awards, and I absolutely love my job!

I also briefly worked at the Chum Bucket (but we don't talk about that).

Education

  • Attendee of Mrs. Puff's Boating School (still working on getting my license!)
  • Highly trained in the art of Krabby Patty preparation
  • Certified in Jellyfishing Safety and Techniques

Hobbies & Interests
